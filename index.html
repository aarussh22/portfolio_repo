What's up?

Hallo Wie geht's? Hi guys I am Aarush Gupta, I'm currently pursuing my Bachelor's degree in Computer Science at Trent University in Peterborough, Ontario. With over eight years of competitive chess experience, I am FIDE 1728 rated and have earned numerous trophies and championship titles throughout my career, including the prestigious title of 2020 National Amateur Category Champion. In addition to playing, I have been teaching chess for the past two years, sharing my knowledge and strategic understanding of the game with aspiring players. My teaching approach combines technical depth with practical insights, shaped by years of high-level competition.

Outside the chessboard, I enjoy staying active by playing basketball and cricket recreationally. Balancing academics, sports, and chess has taught me discipline, time management, and the value of continuous growth—qualities I bring into every aspect of my life. Fitness enthusiast with a serious love for the gym — but an even bigger love for good food. Balancing reps and recipes like a pro. It's a constant tug-of-war, but hey, that's the fun of it! Along with hindi which is my mother tongue and pretty much fluent with english and german languages.
